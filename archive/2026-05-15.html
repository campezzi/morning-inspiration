American Gothic
Grant Wood — 1930

A man and a woman stand before a frame house, the man gripping a pitchfork with the patience of someone who has not been asked to put it down. Grant Wood painted his sister and his dentist — a detail that explains the posture without quite explaining the look. The pitchfork's tines repeat in the stitching of his shirt, which is the kind of thing you notice on the third or fourth viewing. Iowa, 1930, and neither of them is going anywhere.
