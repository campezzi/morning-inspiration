Krazy Kat
George Herriman — 1913

A cat is in love with a mouse who hates her. The mouse expresses contempt by throwing bricks; the cat receives each one as a love letter. Officer Pupp arrests Ignatz every Sunday and discovers nothing about how the world works. Herriman drew this triangle for thirty years in a desert that kept rearranging itself between panels — mesa, snow, night sky, back to dust — as if Coconino County couldn't hold still either.
