Magasin, Avenue des Gobelins
Eugène Atget — 1925

A corset shop window on Avenue des Gobelins, 1925. The mannequins stand behind glass; the street outside — figures, a lamppost, perhaps Atget himself — is reflected across them. He spent thirty years making this kind of picture: Paris before anyone arrived, or just after they'd gone. The glass doesn't choose between the two worlds inside it.
