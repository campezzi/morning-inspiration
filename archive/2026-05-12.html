From Her to Eternity
Nick Cave & The Bad Seeds — 1984
Listen: From Her to Eternity — Nick Cave & The Bad Seeds

The title track opens with percussion that sounds like furniture dragged across concrete — then Cave's voice arrives low, circling, before it tips forward and doesn't stop. This was their debut LP, recorded in London by displaced Australians and a German guitarist from Einstürzende Neubauten; nobody in the room had any intention of staying in one place. Blixa Bargeld's guitar doesn't accompany so much as interrupt. The record covers blues, noir, and something that resists naming, moving between them without asking permission.
