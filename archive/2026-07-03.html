1984 Monaco Grand Prix
Ayrton Senna — 1984
Read: 1984 Monaco Grand Prix

Thirteenth on the grid, in a Toleman nobody expected to trouble the front runners. Ayrton Senna spent the 1984 Monaco Grand Prix cutting through the field in the rain, closing on Alain Prost's McLaren a car length at a time while drivers ahead of him were backing off. He took the lead on the run to the line at the end of lap thirty-two — the same moment the red flag came out to stop the race. Results were frozen to the previous lap, and the win went to Prost. Senna never got to find out what the next one would have looked like.
