Grace
Jeff Buckley — 1994
Listen: Grace — Jeff Buckley

Buckley recorded this in six weeks in 1994, twenty-three years old, playing at a volume that assumed all the time in the world. His "Hallelujah" runs nearly five minutes — so slow it starts to feel like a different song than the one Cohen wrote, built from the same stones but with more weather in it. The guitar on "Lover, You Should've Come Over" sounds like it's being played in a room the size of a cathedral. The album didn't find most of its listeners until after he drowned in the Wolf River Harbor in Memphis, in 1997. It still hasn't finished arriving.
