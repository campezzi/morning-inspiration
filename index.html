Dummy
Portishead — 1994
Listen: Dummy — Portishead

The opening loop on 'Mysterons' arrives before anything else has a chance to announce itself — two notes, low, slightly warped. Dummy was built from drum machines, scratched vinyl, and orchestral samples run through analogue processing until they forgot what they were. Beth Gibbons sings like the words are already a little late. The cover is a still from the band's short film: blurry, grey, a figure barely present — the same atmosphere the record keeps.
