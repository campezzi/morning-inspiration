BurgerTime
Data East — 1982

Peter Pepper spends the entire game walking back and forth across giant hamburger components, which is apparently all it takes to make them fall through a platform and land on the bun below. Three enemies give chase — Mr. Hot Dog, Mr. Egg, Mr. Pickle — each wearing the same blank expression no matter how many times he stuns them with a shake of pepper. Data East released it in 1982 under the working title Hamburger, then had the actual programming subcontracted out to a studio in Akihabara. Nobody in the game explains why the condiments are alive, and nothing in the tone suggests they should.
