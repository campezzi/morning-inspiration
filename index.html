Amazing Stories, August 1928
Frank R. Paul — 1928

The August 1928 cover of Amazing Stories lands you straight in it: a man in an apparatus strapped together from guesswork and optimism, shooting upward, apparently unconcerned about landing. Inside the same issue, Philip Francis Nowlan published "Armageddon 2419 A.D." — Buck Rogers' debut. Frank R. Paul illustrated hundreds of covers like this through the twenties and thirties, each one giving the future the same unworried confidence. The rocket will fire. The suit will hold. The planet will be inhabited, and probably friendly.
