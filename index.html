Church of the Light
Tadao Ando — 1989

The east wall is concrete except for a cross: two slots cut through the full plane, so that light enters the nave and lands on the floor in the shape of the opening. Tadao Ando completed the church in 1989; budget constraints kept the pews as scaffold planks and left the concrete raw. There are no images, no ornament, no colour anywhere in the room. The light does everything.
