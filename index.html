Watch: 1979 French Grand Prix — Villeneuve vs. Arnoux

The last two laps of the 1979 French Grand Prix at Dijon are the best two minutes of wheel-to-wheel racing on record. Gilles Villeneuve in the Ferrari 312T4 and René Arnoux in the Renault RS10 swapped second place four times on the final lap alone — making contact twice, neither once lifting. They were fighting over second; first had been decided. No protest was ever filed. Both drivers climbed out laughing.
