Mirror
Andrei Tarkovsky — 1975
Watch: Mirror — Andrei Tarkovsky

A woman sits on a fence post at the edge of a country road and watches a man disappear into the distance — whether it is memory or dream, Tarkovsky leaves open. Mirror is built from images like this: a buckwheat field in wind, a barn burning at the far end of a field, a mother's face turning toward the camera. The voice on the soundtrack reading poetry is his father's. Between these fragments, newsreel footage of Soviet history passes as if the private and the collective were two currents of the same grief.
