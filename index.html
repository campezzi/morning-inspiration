Voynich Manuscript
Unknown — c. 1420

The plants in this manuscript do not grow anywhere. The script alongside them — looping, fluid, apparently systematic — has not been decoded in a century of trying. Carbon-dated to the early fifteenth century, found by a Polish antiquarian in 1912, the Voynich Manuscript has occupied cryptographers, botanists, and linguists without yielding anything. It may be an invented language, a cipher, an elaborate hoax, or something else entirely. There are 240 pages.
