Endtroducing.....
DJ Shadow — 1996
Listen: Endtroducing..... — DJ Shadow

Every sound on this album is a sample — lifted from crates Josh Davis spent years digging through at a Sacramento record store. The drums hit before you're ready; something else is already arriving underneath. Endtroducing doesn't build to anything — it keeps moving, one layer displacing the next, sounds finally free of the records they came from. You can hear the hunger of the search in it.
